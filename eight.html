Society suffers most not just from the noise of the ignorant, but from the silence of the wise." Note: * A healthy society thrives on truth, knowledge, and responsible speech. * However, two types of people can cause its downfall. * The 1st is the one who knows the truth but chooses to remain silent, either out of fear, indifference, or self-interest. * Their silence allows ignorance, injustice, and corruption to flourish unchecked. * The 2nd is the one who speaks without knowledge, spreading misinformation, confusion, and division. * Their words, though empty of truth, have the power to mislead and manipulate, often shaping public perception and policy in harmful ways. For progress to prevail, * wisdom must find a voice, and ignorance must learn to listen. * Silence in the face of falsehood is complicity, and speaking without understanding is recklessness. * Only when the knowledgeable speak with courage and the uninformed seek wisdom with humility can society truly move forward.
